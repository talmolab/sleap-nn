## Prerequisites

- **Python 3.11, 3.12, or 3.13** (3.14 not yet supported)
- **SLEAP labels file** (`.slp` or `.pkg.slp`) with annotated data
- **GPU recommended** for training (NVIDIA with CUDA, or Apple Silicon with MPS)
